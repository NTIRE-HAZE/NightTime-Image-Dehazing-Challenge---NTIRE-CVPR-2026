## NT-HAZE: NightTime Image Dehazing Challenge Dataset | NTIRE @ CVPR 2026 Workshop

### Project Information

Nighttime image dehazing is considerably more challenging than its daytime counterpart due to glow effects, strong light scattering, and spatially varying haze caused by artificial light sources. Progress in this area has been limited by the lack of realistic paired datasets.

This repository presents **NT-HAZE**, a benchmark dataset for nighttime image dehazing, containing **paired real hazy and haze-free images** captured across **40 diverse indoor nighttime scenes**.

---


### Documents and Reports

The analytical methodology and evaluation results developed during the challenge were submitted to the organizing committee as a formal report.

This repository includes:
- the **official dataset**, and  
- the **challenge report** from the **NTIRE @ CVPR 2026 Workshop**.

All related documents are available in the **root directory**.
